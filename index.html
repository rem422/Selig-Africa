IS Audit & Security Assessment

SELIG identifies the gaps of your current practices vs security policies and standards, assesses your risks, and tests the security of your critical assets and controls.

01

Information System Security Audit

Identify your practice gaps and implement corrective actions.

02

Vulnerability Assessment

Identify and assess the weaknesses of your systems, processes, and operations, and correct them before it's too late.

03

System & Network Pentest

Identify security holes in your networks and systems, and then prioritize corrective actions to fix them to prevent attacks.

04

Web Applications Pentest

Identify and prioritize the vulnerabilities of your web applications regularly and fix them to prevent SQL injection, XSS, brute force attacks, and more.

Cybersecurity Training & Awareness

Cybersecurity training and awareness is imperative to the success of any workplace. We educate and train employees and stakeholders on best practices to prevent or reduce breaches that target insiders, such as phishing.

01

ISO Training & Certification - PECB

This training will allow you to acquire knowledge and skills in Information Security, Governance, Risk and Compliance.

02

Cybersecurity Training & Certification - EC Council

This training will allow you to acquire knowledge and skills in Ethical Hacking, SOC activities and other skills in Cybersecurity.

03

Penetration Testing & Vulnerability Assessment

This training will allow you to acquire knowledge and skills in performing application intrusion tests, network and system.

04

Monitoring & Intrusion Detection

This training will allow you to acquire knowledge and skills in monitoring and intrusion detection in corporate networks.

05

Security Incident Response and Forensics

This training will allow you to acquire knowledge and skills in response to security incidents following security breaches.
